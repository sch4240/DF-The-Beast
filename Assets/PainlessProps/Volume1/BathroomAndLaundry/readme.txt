~Painless Props - Bathroom And Laundry Package~
~by Little Arms Studios, LLC~

This Painless Props Bathroom and Laundry Packages contains low-poly models, textures, materials, and prefabs of various objects you would find in a bedroom. It also contains a showcase scene as an example of how the props might be used to decorate a scene.

List of finished objects:
-Bathroom Mat
-Bathroom Scale
-Bathroom Sink
-Bug Spray
-Comb
-Deoderant
-Dryer
-Electric Razor
-Hairbrush
-Hairdryer
-Handsoap
-Iron
-Ironing Board
-Mirror
-Mouthwash
-Ointment
-Rubberduck
-Shampoo Bottle
-Shower Stall
-Toilet
-Toilet Paper Roll
-Toothbrush Cup
-Toothbrush
-Washing Machine

How to Use:
To place a Painless Prop in your scene, open the Prefabs folder in your Project Pane under Painless Props/Bedroom. Click your desired object prefab and drag it into your scene.
